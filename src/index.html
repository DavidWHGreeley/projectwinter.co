ABOUT THE GAME

When you live among the wolves you must to act like a wolf.
Eight strangers find themselves in a log cabin trapped in the woods in the middle of a harsh winter. In Project Winter your first mistake will be thinking you are one of the sheep. Wolves have the ability to make quick decisions and form emotional attachments but still often need to trust their own instincts and earn loyalty.


Stronger together and weaker apart.
Players must band together to live and escape the harsh arctic environment where venturing off alone could be a death sentence. You will have to interact and work with others to survive although, not everyone may have the best of intentions.


So why do we never see it coming before it is too late?
While the strangers struggle together to survive some are blind to the incognito traitors that lurk amongst them, capable of deadly deceit and treachery.


In Project Winter your goal is to Survive Amongst Wolves.


An 8 player PC game of deception and survival, with a focus on social interaction, hidden roles, and betrayal featuring;

Online Multiplayer/ Cooperative Gameplay (Survivors versus Traitors)
  • Randomly assigned player role (Traitor or Survivor)
  • Limited information, cooperative mechanics, and communication tools for facilitating social interaction between players

Casual Play
  • Simple interactions, small inventory, and minimal crafting
  • Short session length with open exploration
  • Players manage hunger, warmth & health
  • Concise controls with controller support

Customization
  • Unlockable cosmetics for players to create unique identities
  • Tools designed for more immersive ever changing world
